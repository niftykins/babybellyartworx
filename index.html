What is a belly cast?

A cast taken of your pregnant belly and then transformed into a one-of-a-kind piece of art with very soft fairy lighting that you can hang on your baby's bedroom wall.
Step 1

A cast is taken after week 33 to get the best belly shape

Step 2

We come up with a design that's perfect for you (previous designs below for ideas)

Step 3

Your cast is attached to the wooden design and the painting process starts

Step 4

Your memory is finished and ready to hang on your wall

Additional Services

Baby Showers

This is the perfect gift to give at a baby shower, the cast can be done either at the shower with everyone interested placing some casting sheets on the belly or done at a later time chosen by the Mum-to-be.

Already have a cast?

If you have a cast, possibly from a DIY cast kit, it can still be turned into a fantastic design just fill out the form below and mention that you already have a cast and we can discuss what creation we can create for you.

Cast-less Art

If you don't wish to have a design that contains a cast we're still able to work together to create a piece of art to lighten up any room, fairy lights can also be added to act as soft lighting.
