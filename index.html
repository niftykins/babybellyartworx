The Hearthstone Collection Manager

Innkeeper is a new digital Hearthstone assistant that lets you get the most out of your card collection and tools

How does it work?

While you play Hearthstone, Innkeeper runs in the background and automatically scans your card collection and keeps it synced with Hearthpwn.com. It's that simple!

Automatic Syncing

Effortlessly sync your collection. Simply run Innkeeper, and it will automatically pull your collection from hearthstone and send it to Hearthpwn!

Search for Decks

Searching for the best decks has never been easier! Innkeeper syncs your Crafting Dust to Hearthpwn so you can easily find decks you can craft right away!

View your Collection

View breakdowns of your collection by card types, class, and expansion. Share your collection with friends. Innkeeper is the only way to add and show off your gold cards on Hearthpwn.
